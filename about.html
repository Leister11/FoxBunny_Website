Local Web Design for Local Business

FoxBunny Design's purpose is to provide local businesses in the Greenville area with affordable and quality solutions to their online needs
Next

Hello, my name is Josh Leister and I am the owner of FoxBunny Designs.

Greenville has always been my home and I have fallen in love with the small businesses that give it life.
I especially enjoy being able to help those local businesses grow, achieve their goals, and provide the people of Greenville with their products and services. Design, development, and other web technologies are wonderful tools that can be used to showcase and share all of the things that make your business special. Although I find great satisfaction in web design, problem solving, analytics, and art, my absolute favourite part of the process is in meeting local owners, learning how their business works, and listening to them describe their passion and all of the ways their business serves the community.

I believe that local business is the heart of the community and my passion is to spread awareness for those who create beautiful and unique products, services, and ideas.

I look forward to meeting you and working together to make Greenville a better place for everyone.
